    <h1><a id="user-content-user-preferences" class="anchor" href="#user-preferences" aria-hidden="true"><svg aria-hidden="true" class="octicon octicon-link" height="16" version="1.1" viewBox="0 0 16 16" width="16"><path fill-rule="evenodd" d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z"></path></svg></a><a id="user-content-user-preferences" href="#user-preferences"></a>User Preferences</h1><p></p>

<p>From any CyTube channel, you can click the Options link at the top of the page to open a dialog where you can change your personal preferences.  This page explains each of the available options.</p>

<h2><a id="user-content-general" class="anchor" href="#general" aria-hidden="true"><svg aria-hidden="true" class="octicon octicon-link" height="16" version="1.1" viewBox="0 0 16 16" width="16"><path fill-rule="evenodd" d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z"></path></svg></a><a id="user-content-general" href="#general"></a>General</h2>

<p>General interface preferences.</p>

<table><thead>
<tr>
<th>Setting</th>
<th>Description</th>
</tr>
</thead><tbody>
<tr>
<td>Theme</td>
<td>Choose from different colorschemes for the website.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Layout</td>
<td>Choose from different layouts for elements on the page.  Fluid layouts will expand to fill the entire window, while compact layouts will remain the same size.  "Synchtube" layout is the same as the default layout, but mirrored.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Ignore Channel CSS</td>
<td>Don't load custom stylesheets for each channel.  Requires a refresh to take effect.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Ignore Channel JavaScript</td>
<td>Don't load custom scripts for each channel.  The Script Access tab allows you to manage your preferences on a per-channel basis, but if this setting is checked, scripts will be globally disallowed and you will not be prompted to accept them when joining a channel.</td>
</tr>
</tbody></table>

<h2><a id="user-content-playback" class="anchor" href="#playback" aria-hidden="true"><svg aria-hidden="true" class="octicon octicon-link" height="16" version="1.1" viewBox="0 0 16 16" width="16"><path fill-rule="evenodd" d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z"></path></svg></a><a id="user-content-playback" href="#playback"></a>Playback</h2>

<p>Preferences for video playback and the playlist.</p>

<table><thead>
<tr>
<th>Setting</th>
<th>Description</th>
</tr>
</thead><tbody>
<tr>
<td>Synchronize video playback</td>
<td>By default, CyTube attempts to synchronize the video so that everyone is watching at the same time.  Some users with poor internet connections may wish to disable this in order to prevent excessive buffering due to constantly seeking forward.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Synch threshold</td>
<td>The number of seconds your video is allowed to be ahead/behind before it is forcibly seeked to the correct position.  Should be set to at least 2 seconds to avoid buffering problems and choppy playback.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Set wmode=transparent</td>
<td>There's probably no reason to touch this unless you know what you're doing.  Having a non-transparent wmode can cause modals to display behind the video player, but also can cause performance issues in some situations.</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Remove the video player</td>
<td>Automatically remove the video player on page load.  Equivalent to manually clicking Layout-&gt;Remove Video every time you load a channel.</td>
</tr>
